Hi there! I am Robbie and I am currently living in Sacramento, CA with my spouse and our three canine kiddos.

About Me

I've had a passion for tinkering and dissecting the inner workings of technology since childhood. Though I've tried my hand at a few different jobs to further my passion, I've decided to go back to basics in a way and focus on furthering my first outlet for my passion, which is coding.

As an avid hobby starter, I have and will try many hobbies--much to the exasperation of my parter--but whether they remain hobbies is typically 1/5 chance. As for the keepers, those include reading (primarily nonfiction and comics), Magic the Gathering, playing board games, watching horror movies with my partner (a love of which we both share), hiking, cooking, working out, and just playing with my fur babies. I hate being bored, so I try to always be entertained.
